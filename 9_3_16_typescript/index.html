scenario

Built a typescript project in which You are required to categorize people, given their date of birth, and indicate with a true or false flag whether they are of a legal age to Cast a vote as responsible citizen. A person is deemed to be an infant if they are less than 2 years old. Infants cannot Cast vote. A person is deemed to be a child if they are less than 18 years old. Children cannot Cast a vote either. A person is deemed to be an adult if they are more than 18 years of age, and only adults can Cast a vote.
